Introduction and Background
Blockchain technology has become a phenomenon over the past few years and is a revolutionary innovation. It is a digital ledger that records and stores transactions on a network of computers. Blockchain technology has emerged as a revolutionary concept in finance and digital commerce. This decentralized network offers multiple protection and benefits over a centralized network.
Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security and operates independently of a central bank. The first and most well-known cryptocurrency is Bitcoin, which was introduced in 2009 by an unknown person or group using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto. The introduction of Bitcoin, made it possible for thousands of other cryptocurrencies to emerge, each with their own unique features and use cases.
